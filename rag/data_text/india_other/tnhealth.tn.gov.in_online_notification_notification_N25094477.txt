INSTRUCTIONS
 No individual call letters will be issued to candidates.
 Reporting Time: Candidates are required to reach the counselling venue at
least one hour prior to the scheduled start Time, accompanied by one
parent or guardian.
 All candidates reporting to Special category should pay Rs.500 towards
processing fee as Cash. The receipt obtained must be kept safe. If seat is
not allotted, they may not pay Rs. 500 for General Online counselling.
 All costs related to attending the counselling session are the responsibility
of the candidate.
 Essential Documents: Candidates must carry the following original
certificates and documents. Entry to the counselling process will not be
allowed without them.
i. 10th Standard Mark Sheet
ii. 11th Standard Mark Sheet
iii. HSC / Equivalent Examination Mark Sheet
iv. Certificate of proof of study from 6th to 12th Standard (only for
candidates who completed schooling entirely in Tamil Nadu)
v. Transfer Certificate from the last studied school or a Bonafide
Certificate from the institution currently attended
vi. Nativity Certificate
vii. Community Certificate
viii. Community Certificate of either parent (father or mother)
ix. Income Certificate (If applicable)
Important: Due to the high number of candidates invited for counselling, seat
allocation is not guaranteed for all. (2 seats for Ex Servicemen, 2 seats for
Eminent sports persons and 8 seats for Persons with Bench mark Disability who
have underwent Medical examination as advised by the Selection Committee (IM).
Schedule: Candidates must adhere strictly to the counselling date, time, and cut-
off marks specified in the official schedule.
Seat Selection: During the counselling session, candidates can choose only from
the seats available when their turn arrives.
Prohibited Items: Mobile phones and other communication devices are strictly
prohibited inside the counselling hall.
Fee Payment: Candidates are required to bring ₹5,000 in cash as an advance
payment for tuition fees, as outlined in the fee details provided.
