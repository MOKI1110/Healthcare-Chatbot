9. ELIGIBILITY FOR ADMISSION
a. A person possessing the Degree of Homoeopathy (Bachelor of Homoeopathy Medicine and
Surgery) from the Tamil Nadu Dr. MGR Medical University or from any other recognized
University or Board or Medical Institution specified in erstwhile Indian Medicine Central
Council Act 1970 or National Commission for Indian System of Medicine Act, 2020, and
enrolled in Central Counsil of Indian System of Medicine or Tamil Nadu Homoeopathy
Medical Council, with successful completion of CRRI before 31.08.2025 by the NCH /
AIAPGET-2025.
b. The candidates should fulfill the eligibility criteria for applying AIAPGET 2025 Homoeo;
should have qualified in AIAPGET 2025 Homoeo and should have obtained minimum of
marks at 50th percentile. Provided that in respect of candidates belonging to the Scheduled
Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Most Backward Classes and Denotified Community, Backward
Classes (Muslim) and Backward Classes; the minimum marks shall be at 40th percentile; for
candidates with benchmark disabilities specified under the Rights of persons with disabilities
Act, 2016, the minimum marks shall be at 45th percentile for the General Category and 40th
percentile for the Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Most Backward Classes and
Denotified Community, Backward Classes (Muslim) and Backward classes.
Note: Provided further that when sufficient number of candidates in the respective categories fail
to secure minimum marks in the All India AYUSH Post graduate Entrance Test (AIAPGET), as
specified above, held for any academic year for admission to Post – Graduate courses, the Central
Government in consultation with the Council may at its discretion lower the minimum marks
required for admission to Post – Graduate course for candidates belonging to respective
categories and marks so lowered by the Central Government shall be applicable for that academic
year only.
10. Candidates who have passed B.H.M.S from other University should upload an Eligibility

at the time of joining in the college.

time of joining in the college.
13. Candidates must fill in their AIAPGET 2025 (Homoeopathy) Hall Ticket Roll Number and
Marks scored in the relevant fields of the registration form. The original Hall Ticket and Marks
Statement must be produced at the time of admission.

 A refundable advance tuition fee of Rs. 10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand only) at the time

a. Candidates who wish to retain their allotted seats and do not want a change in college are

 Candidates seeking upgradation
 Not-joined candidates
 Unallotted candidates

forfeit the Advance Tuition Fee already paid by the candidate. In addition, he/she should

fulfill the above conditions. Hence, Candidates are advised to thoroughly understand the
bond conditions and evaluate their commitment before accepting admission, as the above
rules will be strictly enforced.
