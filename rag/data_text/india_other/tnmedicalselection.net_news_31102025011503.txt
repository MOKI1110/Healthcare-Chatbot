1. Do not share your login ID, password, or OTP with anyone.
2. Candidates who are choice filling for PG Medical Degree Courses must pay a
non-refundable processing fee of Rs. 1000/- online (via Debit Card/Credit
Card/Net Banking). The choice filling option will be available only after
successful payment.
3. For Choice Filling, all the Candidates should pay a Fee of Rs. 5000/- through online.
4. Candidates should be careful and attentive while selecting their choices.
5. The Seat Matrix for M.D.(Unani) Specialty and colleges will be displayed on the
official websites.
6. The Selection Committee will not be responsible for internet or connectivity issues
faced by the candidates.
7. Admission will be carried out through online counselling only, strictly based on merit
rank and Reservation Rules of Tamil Nadu Government.
8. The step-by-step procedure for online counselling will be published on the official
website.
9. Once a candidate locks the choices, further changes will not be allowed. Changing
the branch/college after choice locking is not allowed for any reason.
10. The Candidate selected for admission should give a self-declaration form at the time
of the reporting to the concerned College. He / she is liable for forfeiture of selection /
admission if suppression of facts / false informations found at any time during or after
the admission to the course.
11. Allotted candidates in Government Colleges must download their provisional
allotment order and report to the concerned Medical College/Institution within the
given date and time.
12. During reporting, Candidates must submit their original certificates along with xerox
copies to the concerned Colleges. If a candidate fails to report to the Head of the
Institution within the stipulated time, the allotment and admission
will be automatically cancelled without prior notice.
