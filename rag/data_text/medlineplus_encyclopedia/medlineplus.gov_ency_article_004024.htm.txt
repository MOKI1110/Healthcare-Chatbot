Drugs that may cause erection problems
To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.
Many medicines and recreational drugs can affect a man's sexual arousal and sexual function. What causes
erection problems
(also called erectile dysfunction or ED) in one man may not affect another man.
Talk to your health care provider if you think that a medicine or drug is having a negative effect on your sexual performance. Never stop taking any medicine without first talking to your provider. Some medicines may lead to life-threatening reactions if you do not take care when stopping or changing them.
