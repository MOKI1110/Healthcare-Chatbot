Scrotum
To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.
The scrotum is a part of a male's body that is located below the
penis
. The scrotum is the sac (pouch) that contains the
testes
, epididymis, and the lower part of the spermatic cord (blood vessels and vas deferens).
