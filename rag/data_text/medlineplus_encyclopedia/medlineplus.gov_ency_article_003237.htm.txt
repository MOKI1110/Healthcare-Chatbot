Whiteheads
To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.
Whiteheads are small, white, raised bumps on the skin. They form when oil and skin collect in the pores. Whiteheads are also known as closed
comedones
and pimples. They are one type of
acne
, often on the face, chest and back.
Related conditions include:
Acne
Blackheads
Alternative Names
Closed comedones; Pimples
