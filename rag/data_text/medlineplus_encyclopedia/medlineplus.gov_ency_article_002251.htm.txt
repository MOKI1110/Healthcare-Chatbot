Macrosomia
To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.
Macrosomia refers to a very large body size. It is often used to describe an oversized fetus.
Information
The most common cause of macrosomia is
diabetes
in the mother. Macrosomia can lead to injury during birth and a greater chance of a
cesarean delivery
(C-section).
