Myositis
To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.
Myositis is an inflammation or swelling of the muscles. It is most often caused by injury, infection, medicines, or an
autoimmune disorder
.
Dermatomyositis
and
polymyositis
are related topics.
Alternative Names
Inflammatory myopathies
