Dermatoses - systemic
To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.
Dermatoses is a general term used to describe any skin defect or lesion on the skin.
Systemic
means it affects the whole body, rather than just one part. Systemic dermatoses may be linked to problems in other organs.
