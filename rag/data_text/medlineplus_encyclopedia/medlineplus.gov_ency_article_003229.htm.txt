Macule
To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.
A macule is a flat, distinct, discolored area of skin. It is a type of skin lesion. It is less than 0.39 inches (in) or 1 centimeter (cm) wide. It usually does not include a change in skin texture or thickness.
