Enzyme markers
To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.
Enzyme markers are tests for specific
enzyme
activity in the body. Diseases or variants passed down through families (inherited) can affect how enzymes work. Some enzymes are affected by several genes.
Test results are usually reported as a percentage of normal enzyme activity.
