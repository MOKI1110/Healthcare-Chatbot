Idiopathic hypercalciuria
To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.
Idiopathic hypercalciuria is the presence of excess calcium in the urine without an identified cause after testing for common causes. This condition can lead to
kidney stones
,
osteoporosis
, or in extreme cases, kidney damage and loss of kidney function.
Alternative Names
Hypercalciuria - idiopathic
