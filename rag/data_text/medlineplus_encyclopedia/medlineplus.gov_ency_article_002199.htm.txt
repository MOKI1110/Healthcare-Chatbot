Alcohol use disorder - resources

Alcohol use disorder is a medical condition. If you think you may have an alcohol use disorder, see a health care provider.
The following organizations are good resources for information on
alcohol use disorder
:
Centers for Disease Control and Prevention --
www.cdc.gov/alcohol/about-alcohol-use/
National Institute on Alcohol Abuse and Alcoholism --
www.niaaa.nih.gov/alcohols-effects-health
Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration --
www.samhsa.gov/find-help/atod
