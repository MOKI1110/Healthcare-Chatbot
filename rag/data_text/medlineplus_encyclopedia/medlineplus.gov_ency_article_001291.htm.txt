Hemoglobinopathy
To use the sharing features on this page, please enable JavaScript.
Hemoglobinopathy is a group of disorders in which there is abnormal production or structure of the
hemoglobin
molecule. It is passed down through families (inherited). Hemoglobin carries oxygen in red blood cells.
This group of disorders includes
hemoglobin C disease
, hemoglobin S-C disease,
sickle cell anemia
, and
thalassemias
.
