Cytochrome b5 reductase
Cytochrome b5 reductase
Cytochrome b5 reductase is an
enzyme
in the blood. It controls the amount of iron in your red blood cells, and helps the cells carry the normal amount of oxygen.
People who do not have enough of this enzyme may develop a condition called
methemoglobinemia
.
