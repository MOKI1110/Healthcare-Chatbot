Breastfeeding - resources
The following organizations are good resources for information on breastfeeding and breastfeeding problems:
The American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (ACOG) --
www.acog.org/womens-health/faqs/breastfeeding-your-baby
La Leche League International --
llli.org
March of Dimes --
www.marchofdimes.org/find-support/topics/parenthood/breastfeeding-best
Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) --
www.cdc.gov/breastfeeding/php/about/index.html
US Department of Agriculture WIC breastfeeding support --
wicbreastfeeding.fns.usda.gov
US Department of Health & Human Services --
womenshealth.gov/breastfeeding
American Academy of Pediatrics --
www.healthychildren.org/English/ages-stages/baby/breastfeeding/Pages/default.aspx
