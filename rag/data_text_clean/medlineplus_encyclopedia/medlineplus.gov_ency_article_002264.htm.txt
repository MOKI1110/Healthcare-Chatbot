Mucosa
Mucosa is moist tissue that lines certain parts of the inside of your body. It is in your:
Nose
Mouth
Lungs
Digestive tract
Urinary and genital tracts
Glands in the mucosa release a thick fluid called mucus.
Alternative Names
Mucous membrane
