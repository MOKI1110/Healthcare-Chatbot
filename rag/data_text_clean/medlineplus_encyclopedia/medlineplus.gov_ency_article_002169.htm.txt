Chemical dependence - resources
Chemical dependence is a medical condition. If you think you may have chemical dependence, see a health care provider.
The following organizations are a good resource for information on medicine and illicit
drug dependence:
National Institute on Drug Abuse --
nida.nih.gov
Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration --
www.samhsa.gov
USA.gov --
www.usa.gov/substance-abuse
US National Library of Medicine, MedlinePlus --
medlineplus.gov/drugsandyoungpeople.html
