Hearing or speech impairment - resources

The following organizations are good resources for information on hearing impairment or speech impairment:

  • Alexander Graham Bell Association for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing -- agbell.org
  • American Speech-Language-Hearing Association -- www.asha.org/public
  • National Institute on Deafness and Other Communication Disorders (NIDCD) -- www.nidcd.nih.gov
  • Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) -- www.cdc.gov/hearing-loss-children/
  • American Society for Deaf Children -- deafchildren.org
  • Hearing Loss Association of America -- www.hearingloss.org
